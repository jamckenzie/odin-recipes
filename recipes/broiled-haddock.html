Simple Broiled Haddock

Description

A light and flaky fish readily available in New England, this recipe spices up its mild flavor and is easy to prepare

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 pounds haddock filets
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter, cut in small pieces
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Set oven rack about 6 inches from heat source, preheat oven's broiler
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray
  3. Arrange haddock fillets on prepared baking sheet
  4. Mix the onion and garlic powders, paprika, salt, and the black and cayenne peppers in a small bowl
  5. Sprinkle seasoning over haddock, then dot with pieces of butter
  6. Broil in preheated oven until fish is easily flaked with a fork, about 6-8 minutes. Serve with lemon wedges
